Added support for font features and ligatures, RTL languages like Arabic and Hebrew, and text shaping with Harfbuzz. Three new built-in font bundles ship for non-Latin scripts: `tegaki/fonts/suez-one` (Hebrew), `tegaki/fonts/amiri` (Arabic), and `tegaki/fonts/klee-one` (Japanese — kana, JP punctuation, and Kyōiku grade 1–2 kanji).
